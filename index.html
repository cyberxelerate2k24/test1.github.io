About Symposium



The RMKCET Cyber Security Symposium "CyberXelerate" is an exciting, dynamic event that unites enthusiasts and experts in the field. Registration is free, allowing participants to take full advantage of the diverse competitions available. Engage in thrilling paper presentations to showcase your research, participate in debugging challenges to test your coding prowess, and demonstrate your penetration testing skills in the capture the flag competition. Show off your creative abilities in web designing contests and stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes, adding an extra layer of motivation to demonstrate your cybersecurity expertise. Moreover, this enriching cybersecurity experience is not just about technical competitions, participants can also look forward to non-technical events that add a touch of diversity and fun to the symposium. To sweeten the deal, food will be provided, ensuring that participants can focus on the challenges at hand without worrying about their hunger. Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to showcase your skills, learn from experts, and enjoy a memorable experience. Register soon to secure your spot and immerse yourself in the world of CyberXelerate.
